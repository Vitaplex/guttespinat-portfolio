## Acting out of emotion
A key point in developing a more grounded mindset, is to realize - and know, even in the worst moments - that **your thoughts and your feelings are not you, and do not define you!**
* Anger is not you 
* Anxiety is not you
* Fear is not you
* Jealousy is not you
* Happiness is not you

Emotions are no more than reactions arising within you, coming and going like waves in the ocean.

## Finding Balance
Balance can be described as being "in the middle of", being in the middle ground. This means, to know what balance is, you have to have seen both ends of the spectrum. If you do not explore yourself, you will not know where the boundaries are, thus, balance cannot be achieved. Exploring yourself, learning where your boundaries are, and also pushing them, allows you to see yourself from a new perspective. You might even feel proud of yourself.
